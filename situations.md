## Capture and extract

- **Goal:** Secure an artifact or target and extract to a safe zone.
- **Setup:** Place the artifact in a guarded area; define extraction point(s) on a different map region.
- **Rules:** Carry limits (heavier items slow movement), alarms attract NPCs, stealth or force both viable.
- **Scoring:** Successful extraction with artifact; partial credit for reaching extraction under pursuit.
- **Constraints:** Investiture detection, limited disguises, lock difficulty scales with tech level (fabrials vs. mechanized locks).
- **Optional twists:** Decoy artifacts, cursed items (slow healing), extraction point relocation after alarm.
